Eagle Valley news
[LCCN: sn96088408]
Richland, Oregon
Richland, Or

 

In 1897 the eastern Oregon town of Richland was established in response to the growing mining and railroad industries.  The Eagle Valley news, [LCCN: sn96088408], started in the early nineteen-teens and was published weekly and cost $1.50 for an annual subscription.  The newspaper ran for a few years and on July 10, 1919 an article ran on the front page announcing the indefinite suspension of the newspaper due to the publisher’s medical issues.

 

Prepared with reference to:
Bailey, Barbara Ruth (1982). Main Street: Northeastern Oregon. Oregon Historical Society. p. 53. ISBN 0875950736.
